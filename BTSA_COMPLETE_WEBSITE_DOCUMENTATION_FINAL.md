## Executive Summary

Build To Shelter Africa (BTSA) is a pan-African NGO uniting professionals across the continent to address housing poverty, youth empowerment, and environmental sustainability through the UN Sustainable Development Goals framework.

**Mission:** Empowering Communities, Building Homes, Protecting the Planet

**Website Purpose:**
- Launch fundraising for Liberia Youth Empowerment & Housing Initiative ($26,000 goal)
- Recruit volunteers across Africa (70% women participation target)
- Establish credibility through radical transparency
- Create immersive, story-driven digital experience
- Scale platform for future projects across Africa

**Design Philosophy:** "Journey of Transformation" - An immersive, story-driven experience that takes visitors from crisis to solution, inspiring action through authentic African visual storytelling.

## Website Concept & User Experience

### Design Philosophy: "Journey of Transformation"

An immersive, story-driven website that takes visitors through the journey from crisis to thriving community, using interactive scrolling experiences and authentic African visual storytelling.

**Core Principles:**
1. **Emotional Connection** - Every interaction builds empathy and inspiration
2. **Radical Transparency** - Show everything: budgets, challenges, progress
3. **Dignity-First** - Authentic storytelling that respects beneficiaries
4. **Action-Oriented** - Clear pathways to donate, volunteer, partner
5. **Mobile-First** - Optimized for African internet (3G/4G)
6. **Pan-African Pride** - Celebrate African solutions to African challenges
